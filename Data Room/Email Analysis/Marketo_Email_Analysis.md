### Strengths
1. **High Deliverability on Targeted Campaigns** - AM intros at 95-96%, transactional emails at 95-97%
2. **Strong Open Rates** - 27-35% open rates significantly above industry average
3. **Geographic Segmentation** - Well-organized regional targeting (Alberta, Florida, Ontario)
4. **Growing Database** - Consistent new lead acquisition with Feb 2026 showing peak activity
5. **Effective Nurture Sequences** - Email 2 and 3 show improved deliverability after list cleaning
6. **Robust Transactional System** - Payment notifications have 95%+ deliverability

### Areas to Monitor
1. **Wide-Net Blast Deliverability** - "3 Rental Mistakes" at 66.7% indicates cold list issues
2. **AM Cleanup Campaign** - 68-75% deliverability suggests stale account data
3. **Hard Bounces** - 2,914 hard bounces on main promotional campaigns
4. **Soft Bounces** - 5,210 soft bounces indicate potential deliverability issues
5. **Newsletter [2025]** - Currently shows 0 members, needs activation
6. **Legacy Programs** - mkto_00742786 is empty and should be archived

### Recommendations

#### Immediate Actions
1. **Clean Hard Bounces** - Remove 2,914+ hard bounces from promotional lists
2. **Archive mkto_00742786** - Empty legacy program cluttering workspace
3. **Review AM Cleanup Lists** - Low deliverability suggests outdated data
4. **Investigate Wide-Net Blast Strategy** - 66.7% deliverability on "3 Rental Mistakes" needs review

#### Short-Term Improvements
1. **Activate Newsletter [2025]** - Currently shows 0 members
2. **Implement List Hygiene** - Regular cleaning before wide-net campaigns
3. **A/B Test Subject Lines** - Improve open rates on promotional emails
4. **Segment Cold vs Warm Contacts** - Different approaches for different engagement levels

#### Long-Term Strategy
1. **Update Nurture Campaign** - April 2018 nurture is outdated, create new sequences
2. **Automate Bounce Management** - Set up workflows to suppress bad addresses
3. **Enhance Abandoned Cart** - Currently low volume, opportunity to scale
